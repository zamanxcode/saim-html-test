Bangladesh Heavy Metal History

Bangladesh e heavy metal music er utpotti 1980-er decade e, jakhon "Warfaze", "Rockstrata" o "In Dhaka" moto band gulo underground scene e rise korte thake. Ei shilpi ra desher traditional sangeet culture er biruddhe ekti natun rebellion niye ashchilo. Warfaze 1986-e tader formation diye metal scene ke mainstream e tulte thake. Erpor "Cryptic Fate", "Artcell", o "Aurthohin" metal ke notun dimension e niye jay. Artcell er album Oniket Prantor ke Bangladesh er progressive metal movement er milestone bola hoy. University campus, small gigs, ebong online community e metal er fan base barte thake. Er moddhe lyrics gula besh often philosophical o protest-based thake. Government restrictions, lack of sponsorship o stage er obhab thaka shotto heavy metal bangladeshi youth der moddhe ekta cult-er moto spread kore. Notun generation er bands gulo jemon "Trainwreck", "Bay of Bengal" o "Mechanix" ekhon international scene eo name tule niye jachhe. Metal ekhon sudhu ekta music genre na, ekta movement.
